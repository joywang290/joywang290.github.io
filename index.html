My interest in Data Science is to further utilize the state-of-the art techiniques of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and image analysis to improve patient's quality of life, provide insights into decision-making for healthcare professionals, and help increase healthcare accessibility. The passion for improving the quality of life and preventing diseases drives me on my career path to pursue my goal, and now, I am ready to make a positive impact with his professional knowledge and skills.

Work

Mental Health in Tech Survey

For this final project of a class, I built this website using the dataset The Mental Health in Tech Survey 2014 downloaded from Kaggle to discover the most influential factors that affect employee's attitudes toward mental health diseases.

  • Conducted data cleaning, bivariate and multivariate analysis to discover the strongest predictor and created a k-nearest-neighbors prediction model in python.
  • Used Flask as the framework, HTML and CSS to format the layout of the webpage.
  • Fetched data through API by JavaScript and plotted the graph using Plotly.
